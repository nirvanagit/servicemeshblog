## About Service Mesh Blog

**Service Mesh Blog** is a practitioner-focused technical blog covering the service mesh ecosystem — from day-zero architecture decisions to production war stories.

### What We Cover

- **Istio** — control plane deep dives, ambient mesh, Ztunnel, and Waypoint proxies
- **Envoy Proxy** — filter chains, xDS APIs, WASM extensions, and performance tuning
- **eBPF Networking** — Cilium, Tetragon, and kernel-bypass approaches
- **Zero Trust** — mTLS, SPIFFE/SPIRE, certificate management, and OPA integration
- **Observability** — distributed tracing (Tempo, Jaeger), metrics (Prometheus), and service graphs

### Who This Is For

Platform engineers, SREs, and architects building and operating Kubernetes-based microservices platforms.
